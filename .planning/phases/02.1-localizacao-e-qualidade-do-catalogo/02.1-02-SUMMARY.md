## Decisions Made

- RAWG sync does not write time estimates or availability by default because absent or untrusted RAWG data must not overwrite curated facts.
- Dry-run mode is intentionally non-mutating and therefore does not create sync audit rows.
- Live provider integration is optional for v1; QUEUE/2 curated seeds remain the published source.
