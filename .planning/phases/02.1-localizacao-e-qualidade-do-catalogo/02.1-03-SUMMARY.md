## Decisions Made

- The detail page is the only place with full provenance rows; cards stay dense for browsing.
- QUEUE/2-reviewed Portuguese copy does not invent a link. It appears as text unless the row has a real external source.
- `data-freshness` values are visual hooks only; every row also includes human-readable state text.
