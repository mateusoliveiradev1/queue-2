## Decisions Made

- `arquivo` is supported by policy/read contracts, but active `todas` excludes `zerado` and `dropado`.
- `match` ordering uses deterministic compatibility signals available in SQL: main-flow eligibility, coop confirmation, platform availability, reliable time, verified availability, then recent/name tie-breakers.
- `Proximos da fila` is a short bounded slice from active statuses, while the page is independently filtered and paginated.
