# Phase 01.1 Context

## Why This Phase Exists

Phase 1 is closed for implementation and gate progression. The user accepted the auth visual direction but asked for targeted polish before moving into Phase 2:

- The `/2` mark on auth screens should return to the landing page.
- The isolated `/2` in the form panel may be weaker than the full brand in some contexts.
- The left public/auth panel can feel more intentional and less empty.
- The home is not the final landing page; the final public landing remains Phase 7.

## Scope

In scope:

- Interim `/` home polish.
- Public auth/verification/recovery/pairing layout refinement.
- Brand navigation from public screens back to `/`.
- Focus, mobile fit and accessible names.
- Tests that prove the new visible contracts.

Out of scope:

- Database, auth core behavior, Better Auth configuration, RLS, migrations.
- RAWG catalog or library features.
- Final launch landing, pricing/marketing sections or Phase 7 storytelling.
- Real email provider and Neon restore rehearsal launch evidence.

## Decision

Treat Phase 01.1 as a narrow inserted phase. It improves first impression and navigation without changing the completed Phase 1 security surface.
